Interview Instructions

Please read the following instructions carefully before beginning your interview:

  1. Review the questions: You will be asked one question at a time. Each question appears in a dropdown menu.
  2. Start Recording: Click the “Start Recording” button to begin answering the question verbally.
  3. Stop Recording: Once you finish speaking, click the “Stop Recording” button.
  4. Auto Progress: After each response, the next question will be automatically selected for you.
  5. Do not refresh the page: Doing so may cause all recorded responses to be lost.
  6. Complete All Questions: Continue until all 4 questions have been answered.
  7. Finalize: When you are done, click the “Finalize Interview” button to submit your answers for analysis.
  8. Privacy: Your responses will be securely saved and analyzed, and a detailed feedback report will be generated.
